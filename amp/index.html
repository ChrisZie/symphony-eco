Who We Are

Who We Are

We are a Canadian and a German who met while managing and volunteering at a Non-Profit Organization.

Between us, we have traveled through almost 100 countries, passionately growing our knowledge about environmental conservation and ecotourism with each new country.

With our passion to drive us and our knowledge to guide us, we are determined to live a life of value, doing our very best to protect the vulnerable lives of all animals and plants.

What We Know

What We Know

We share a wonderfully useful combination of experience, expertise, and education.

We are professional Technologists and Project Managers. We have degrees and certifications in Environmental Sciences, Business, Technology, and Project Management.

We have years of experience in managing Non-Profit Organizations, Government Agencies, and Tourism Businesses using Technology, Marketing, Volunteers, and Fundraising.

Why We Help

Why We Help

The short answer: because we absolutely will not stand by when the environment is in such peril.

We have seen, so many times now, that NGOs and Governments have determination but always find themselves limited by the exact same issues: Brand Awareness, Money, and People.

This is where we come in. With all of the knowledge and training we have to address these issues specifically, it is unacceptable to us to not provide all of the assistance possible.
